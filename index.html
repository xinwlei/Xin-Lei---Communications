Flight Attendent

As a flight attendant, we are responsible for attending to the safety and comfort needs of passengers aboard on the flight. We perform a range of tasks including pre-flight safety checks - this is done before passenger boarding of every new flight to ensure cleanliness of the plane, water and food supplies are fully stocked, and to provide a comfortable environment overall. As passengers board, our tasks can range from anywhere of welcoming the passengers, verifying tickets, and directing passengers to their seats. Once all passengers have boarded and the doors have closed, we demonstrate emergency procedures - this is usually done during the beginning of the flight before take-off to ensure all boarded passengers are aware of the actions that need to be taken in case of emergency. This includes the operation of oxygen masks and exit locations. During the flight, we prepare and serve beverages, snacks and food to passengers, both complimentary and requested by passengers. We are here to answer any questions and/or concerns that passengers may have regarding the flight, travels routes, arrival times, and more. The main task that we perform every flight is checking that passengers have their seatbelts fastened during take-off and landing, and any time when the seatbelt sign is on.

I love my job, and I would like to share it with the world so more people can learn about what it is like being a flight attendent as well.

As long as you love what you do, then you have made it in life.
